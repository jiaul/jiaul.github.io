Academic Interests

 I try to work on Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Big Data Processing, and ML driven Artificial Intelligence.
My research objective is to develop mathematical and computational models on data that stands on comprehensible theoretical foundation and validated by rigorous experimental evaluation.
I enjoy writing non-trivial and efficient computer program for large data.

Teaching is equally enjoyable to me. Broadly, I love to teach the subjects on Machine Learning/Deep Learning, Mathematical Foundations for ML/Data Science, Computer Programming, Data Structure and Algorithm Design.
